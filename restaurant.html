About Us

Welcome to Bella Vista, a culinary haven where Italian tradition meets modern elegance. Since opening our doors in 2010, we've dedicated ourselves to creating unforgettable dining moments.

Our Mission

We strive to connect people through exceptional food, heartfelt hospitality, and a cozy, elegant atmosphere.

Signature Dishes

Try our beloved truffle mushroom risotto or our crispy wood-fired Neapolitan pizzas—made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Our Team

Led by Chef Marco D’Amico, our team brings over 20 years of international culinary experience to your table.

Our Values

We are committed to sustainability, supporting local farmers, and creating a welcoming space for all our guests.

Award-Winning Dining

Recognized as Nairobi’s “Best Fine Dining Restaurant” for three consecutive years.
